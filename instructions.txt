~ How to play ~

Use the arrow keys to move around the world

w - Swing the sword north
a - Swing the sword west
s - Swing the sword south
d - Swing the sword east

q - Special attack

Your health will regenerate if you don't get hit for a while
Slaining monsters can sometime raise your attack, defense or maximum health

Goal: Find the villain and defeat it!

Press 'c' to continue
